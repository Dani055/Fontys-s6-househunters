For my walking skeleton, I tried to implement all aspects of my C2 diagram (refer to architecture document) to prove that my architecture can work.
The walking skeleton consists of all of my microservices configured and running inside a docker environment.
A message broker is also present, with a working proof of concept where my listing microservice publishes and event and is then picked up by only 1 instance of my bid microservice, since all instances of my bid microservices listen to the same queue. RabbitMQ dispatches the messages on a round-robin mechanism to all listeners of the queue.
